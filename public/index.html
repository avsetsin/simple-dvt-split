0x split contract

A contract that distributes rewards to participants. The shares among the participants will be divided evenly. The controller address will be set to zero address and distributorFee will be 0.

Connect with the multisig address to automatically fill the list of owners.

Wrapper contract (cluster reward address)

The contract that is specified as the rewardAddress of a node operator. Wraps stETH rewards to wstETH and sends it to the rewards splitter contract. The contract may take a fee on rewards, so it's important to choose the right cluster type. The amount of fee and the fee recipient will depend on this.
